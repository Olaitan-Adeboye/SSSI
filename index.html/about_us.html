About Us

Shelter Story Song Initiative (SSSI) exists to create safe, healing spaces where young people can
discover themselves, overcome adversity, and live purposeful lives.
We believe in the power of community, storytelling, and resilience to nurture growth and transformation.

Our Mission

To create safe, healing spaces and empowerment programmes that help young people overcome adversity, discover themselves, and build purposeful lives through shelter, storytelling, emotional support, and community-based initiatives.

Our Vision

We envision a world where young people have safe spaces to grow roots, find their voice, and live with meaning.

Our Core Values

Community – It takes a village to raise and empower a child; collective care builds stronger futures.
Compassion – We create safe, healing spaces rooted in empathy and understanding.
Purpose – We guide young people to live meaningful, intentional lives.
Resilience – We empower young people to overcome adversity with strength and hope.
